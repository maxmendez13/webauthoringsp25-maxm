Where Movies are Set vs Where Movies are Shot

Many of the films that are inspired by or take place in the Northeast are not necessarily shot there. Let's take a look at if the setting and where they were actually shot line up for these films!

Long Island Blairstown Manhattan Hackensack Vancouver Toronto Toms River Haddonfield South Pasadena

Filming Location Movie Setting Both Set + Film Location

The films Jason Takes Manhattan (1989) and American Psycho (2000) take place in Manhattan.

Amityville Horror (1979) takes place on Long Island, NY

Bride of Chucky (1998) takes place across New York and New Jersey but the finale of the movie takes place in Hackensack

Friday the 13th (1980) takes place at the fictional Camp Crystal Lake but was shot in Blairstown, NJ

Halloween (1978) takes place in Haddonfield, IL (fictional) but was inspired by Haddonfield, NJ

Vancouver, British Columbia was where Jason Takes Manhattan was filmed.

Amityville Horror was shot in Toms River, NJ

Halloween was shot in South Pasadena, CA

Both Bride of Chucky and American Psycho were filmed in Toronto, Ontario
